Overview
To better evaluate and improve the generalization of learning-based driving system, we introduce an open-ended and highly configurable driving simulator called PGDrive. PGDrive can generate infinite driving scenes through procedural generalization, which can benifit the research on the generalization of learning system. Please visit github.com/decisionforce/pgdrive to enjoy PGDrive!
